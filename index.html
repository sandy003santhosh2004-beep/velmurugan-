About Us

A Legacy of Pure & Traditional Oil Making

Velmurugan Oil Shop has been serving pure, cold-pressed oils for over 15 years. Our journey began with a simple belief — every family deserves access to healthy, chemical-free cooking oils made the traditional way.

We use the ancient Marachekku (wood-pressed) method to extract oils at low temperatures, ensuring all the natural flavors, aromas, and nutrients are preserved in every drop.

Our Process

From Seed to Oil — The Traditional Way

Every step is carefully handled to ensure maximum purity and nutrition

01

Seed Selection

We hand-pick the finest quality seeds directly from trusted local farmers across Tamil Nadu

02

Sun Drying

Seeds are naturally sun-dried to achieve the perfect moisture level for optimal oil extraction

03

Cold Pressing

Using traditional Marachekku (wood press), oils are extracted at low temperatures to retain nutrients

04

Natural Filtration

The oil is naturally filtered and settled without any chemical processing or refinement

05

Fresh Packaging

Packed in food-grade bottles and delivered fresh, ensuring maximum shelf life and quality
